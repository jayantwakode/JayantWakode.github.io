I am Jayant

I began my academic journey in Aerospace Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, but faced an ethical dilemma that made me reassess my path. This led me to pursue a B.Sc. in Sports Science at the University of Münster, aligning my career with my passion for sports, human movement, and well-being.

Alongside my studies, I serve as deputy to the office manager at the Münster branch of the German Alpine Club (DAV), where I am fortunate to contribute to mountain sports every day.

What I like to do

My professional and academic interests focus on applying technological developments to medical sciences, with a particular emphasis on sports.

In my free time, I enjoy climbing, slacklining, photography in nature, and learning about the animal world through documentaries.

I am passionate about teaching climbing and share this through courses with the German Alpine Club (DAV). Additionally, I provide consultation on Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) in climbing, putting my PPE Inspector certification into practice.

I am fluent in English, German, Hindi, and Marathi.
